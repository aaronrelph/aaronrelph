The web reading experience was one of the first projects I took on at gumroad. Previously we were using an off the shelf reader that had served it's purpose but wasn't as full featured as we would have liked and a pain to maintain. So we built our own. Goal was to build an experience for the web that was just as powerful as the experience in a native app like kindle or ibooks.
