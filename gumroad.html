Work

Gumroad.com

2014 - Present

At Gumroad we’re building a company that helps creators earn a living doing what they love. For creators we build features that help grow their audience, sell their products, and communicate with their consumers. For the consumer we build products that let them consume that content in the best way possible through our web consumption experience or in our native apps.

Over the last year we’ve shipped a ton of stuff. This is taste of what I’ve worked on there.
